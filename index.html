5. Sampling

Section Description Here

Field Level Relevant Standard Definition Response Options Controlled Vocabulary Notes
Country Treatment effect DDI 2.5 Country or countries where the study was implemented. Text-CV ISO country codes Add "other" and "did not report" options.
Sub-national location Treatment effect DDI 2.5 Geographic location sampled within the country. Open text — Examples: villages in Busia, cities in Indonesia.
Unit of randomization Arm AEA Registry, ClinicalTrials.gov Unit at which randomization was done and treatment assigned. Open text and Text-CV From Cavanagh et al. (2023) Use controlled vocab from Appendix B and refine in pilot.
Unit of analysis Treatment effect ClinicalTrials.gov Unit at which treatment effect was measured. Open text and Text-CV From Cavanagh et al. (2023) Compare to unit of randomization to assess mismatch.
Eligibility of experimental population Study DDI 2.5, ClinicalTrials.gov Criteria for inclusion/exclusion in the experimental sample. Open text — Record for both unit of randomization and analysis.
Recruitment of experimental sample Study or arm ClinicalTrials.gov Key info about how the sample was recruited. Open text — Include if recruitment was random or non-random.
Sampling from recruited Treatment effect ClinicalTrials.gov How the unit was sampled after recruitment. Text-CV Randomized, Non-randomized, N/A Specify unit of analysis and unit of randomization.
Deviation from sample inclusion Treatment effect ClinicalTrials.gov Any dropouts prior to treatment assignment. Open text — Used to monitor reporting completeness.
Baseline average female share Study ClinicalTrials.gov Proportion of females in the study population. Numeric — Control group average if baseline unavailable.
Baseline average age Study ClinicalTrials.gov Average age of the study population. Numeric — Control group average if baseline unavailable.
6. Intervention

Section Description Here

Field Level Relevant Standard Definition Response Options Controlled Vocabulary Notes
Name of intervention Treatment arm ClinicalTrials.gov Descriptive name used to refer to the intervention arm. Open text — Used for labeling and comparison of arms.
Details of intervention Treatment arm ClinicalTrials.gov, AEA Registry Details that distinguish the intervention from others. Open text — Includes dosage, duration, method, etc.
Name of arm Treatment arm ClinicalTrials.gov Label used for each group/arm in the study. Open text — Used for assigning outcome and estimates.
Number of randomization units per arm Treatment arm — Number of clusters or individuals per arm. Numeric — Used to assess sample size per treatment.
Mapping of intervention to arms Treatment arm ClinicalTrials.gov How interventions are assigned to each arm. Format — Important for cross-referencing.
Intervention assignment strategy Study ClinicalTrials.gov Strategy used to assign participants to interventions. Text-CV Simple, complete, stratified, cluster Used to evaluate internal validity.
Timeline of intervention Treatment arm AEA Registry Start and end date of intervention. Date (YYYY-MM-DD) — Used to align with outcome collection.
