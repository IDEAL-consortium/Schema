Field Level Relevant Standard Definition Response Options Controlled Vocabulary Notes

DDI 2.5: Universe: The group of persons or other elements that are the object of research and to which any analytic results refer. Name of arm Treatment arm ClinicalTrials.gov Label used for each group/arm in the study. Open text ‚Äî Used for assigning outcome and estimates.
Number of randomization units per arm Treatment arm ‚Äî Number of clusters or individuals per arm. Numeric ‚Äî Used to assess sample size per treatment.
